#TLR Pathway
TOLL - Receptor
TLR - Receptor
MYD88 - Canonical adaptor molecule for TLR/Toll
TCAM1 - Alternative co-adaptor molecule for TLR/Toll
TCAM2 - Alternative adaptor molecule for TLR/Toll
SARM1 - Negative regulator for TCAM2-dependent TLR/Toll signalling
TRAF - Signalling intermediate
TANK - Regulator of TRAFs

#Transcription factors and associated molecules
IRF - Transcription factor
NFKB - Transcription factor
IKK - Repressor of IKB
NEMO - Repressor of IKK
IKB - Repressor of NFKB
